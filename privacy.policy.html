Who I am

Controller: Viacheslav Gilevich (“I”, “me”). I develop and publish the Shock Diamonds app as an individual.

Contact: gilevich@qalti.com

Summary

  • No accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs.
  • I may receive Apple diagnostics crash reports if you have opted in at the OS level.
  • If you email me, I process your email address and the content you send to respond.

What I don’t collect

  • No in-app tracking or profiling.
  • No location data.
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data (unless you explicitly choose a file to import).

What I may process

Support emails

If you contact gilevich@qalti.com, I’ll receive your email address, message content, and any attachments. I use this only to respond and improve the app.

Crash reports & diagnostics

If you’ve enabled sharing diagnostics with developers, Apple may provide crash logs and device basics (e.g., device model, OS version, app version, timestamps, stack traces). I use these to debug stability issues. I don’t combine this with other data to identify you.

Payments

All purchases/refunds are processed by Apple. I don’t receive your payment details.

On-device processing & permissions

The app runs a real-time 3D simulation locally. When you import a 3D model (e.g., USDZ), it’s accessed through the system file picker and processed on-device. The files are not uploaded to me.

Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Legitimate interests — operate, secure, and improve the app (e.g., using opt-in crash reports).
  • Consent — when you email me and voluntarily provide information.

Data retention

  • Support emails: kept as long as needed to handle your request and for a reasonable period to improve support quality. You can request deletion anytime.
  • Crash reports: retained according to Apple’s diagnostics systems; I generally don’t control their retention on Apple’s side.

Sharing

I don’t sell or share personal data. I may disclose information if required by law or to protect my rights, users, or the public.

International transfers

Support emails may be routed through servers outside your country by my email provider. Where applicable, I rely on appropriate safeguards provided by those service providers.

Children’s privacy

The app doesn’t collect personal data from users. If a child contacts me by email, a parent/guardian should supervise. If I learn I’ve received a child’s personal information without appropriate consent, I’ll delete it.

Your rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, UK, or similar jurisdictions, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of your personal data, and to object to processing. You may also complain to your local supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, email: gilevich@qalti.com.

California (CPRA)

I do not “sell” or “share” personal information as defined by California law, and I do not use cross-context behavioral advertising.
