Frequently asked questions

What is Shock Diamonds?

It’s an educational visualization of compressible gas flow that shows how Mach diamonds form in 3D. It is not a professional engineering or safety tool.

Does the app track me?

No. There is no tracking, analytics, or user account system.

Can I add my own 3D model?

Yes. You can import a custom 3D model. We fully support USDZ; other formats may work, but USDZ provides the most reliable results.

Do you offer refunds or manage subscriptions here?

There are no in-app subscriptions or accounts. Purchases and refunds are handled by the App Store according to Apple’s policies.

Troubleshooting

Performance feels slow or device gets warm

Shock Diamonds runs a real-time 3D compressible-gas simulation and rendering pipeline. This is computationally intensive and can tax mobile GPUs/CPUs, which may reduce frame rate or generate noticeable heat.

  • If your device becomes too warm, close the app and allow it to cool before continuing.
  • Close other apps to free memory and GPU.
  • Ensure Low Power Mode is off for best performance.
  • Restart the device if it’s been a while since the last reboot.

The app crashes or freezes

  • Update to the latest version of Shock Diamonds and iOS/iPadOS/macOS.
  • Try a device restart. If it persists, email us with steps to reproduce.

The simulation becomes unstable

  • Lower the Mach speed or the pressure ratio and try again.
  • Extremely aggressive settings can push the simulation outside stable ranges.

Model import fails or looks incorrect

  • We currently fully support USDZ files.
  • We also attempt to load other common 3D formats the best we can.
  • If something doesn’t look right, try a different model, or convert your model to USDZ and re-import.
